Library Membership

Join our community of learners and researchers. As a member of NSTU Central Library, you'll gain access to our extensive collection of books, journals, digital resources, and exclusive services to support your academic and professional growth.

Membership Types

Student Membership

  • For all enrolled NSTU students
  • Free of charge
  • Borrow up to 5 books for 14 days
  • Access to digital resources

Requires: Valid student ID card

Faculty Membership

  • For NSTU faculty members
  • Free of charge
  • Borrow up to 10 books for 30 days
  • Priority access to new arrivals

Requires: Faculty ID and department approval

External Membership

  • For alumni and researchers
  • Annual fee: 1000 BDT
  • Borrow up to 3 books for 14 days
  • Reading room access

Requires: National ID and reference letter

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the membership last?

Student and faculty memberships are valid as long as you're affiliated with NSTU. External memberships are valid for one year from the date of registration and can be renewed.

Can I renew my books online?

Yes, you can renew your books online through our library portal, provided there are no holds on the items. Each book can be renewed up to 2 times.

What are the library opening hours?

The library is open Sunday to Thursday from 8:30 AM to 9:00 PM, and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We're closed on Fridays and public holidays.

Is there a late fee for overdue books?

Yes, there's a late fee of 10 BDT per book per day for students and faculty, and 20 BDT per book per day for external members.
