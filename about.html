About NSTU Central Library

Empowering academic excellence through knowledge resources, research support, and innovative learning spaces.

Our Mission

The NSTU Central Library serves as the intellectual hub of Noakhali Science and Technology University, providing comprehensive resources and services to support teaching, learning, and research activities across all disciplines.

We are committed to fostering a culture of lifelong learning, facilitating access to information, and creating an environment conducive to academic exploration and discovery.

Our Journey & Vision

History

2006

Establishment

NSTU Central Library founded alongside the university with a modest collection of 5,000 volumes.

2012

Digital Expansion

Launched our first digital catalog system and subscribed to major academic databases.

2018

New Facility

Moved to our current state-of-the-art building with expanded study spaces and technology hubs.

2023

Modernization

Implemented RFID technology and 24/7 digital access to resources.

Vision

Our Vision

To be a world-class academic library that drives innovation, supports cutting-edge research, and cultivates a community of lifelong learners.

Strategic Goals

  • Expand digital resources and remote access capabilities
  • Enhance research support services for faculty and students
  • Develop innovative learning spaces that foster collaboration
  • Promote information literacy across the university community
  • Strengthen partnerships with academic departments
