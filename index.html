About Our Library

The NSTU Central Library serves as the academic heart of Noakhali Science and Technology University, providing comprehensive resources and services to support teaching, learning, and research activities.

With over 50,000 volumes, digital resources, and state-of-the-art facilities, we are committed to fostering intellectual growth and academic excellence within our university community.
